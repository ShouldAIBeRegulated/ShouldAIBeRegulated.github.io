Recommended Reading

Here's a set of articles to get you started. Below, you'll find five articles extensively debating whether AI development should continue, along with 5 more articles detailing the nature, current state, and capabilities of AI based technology and their implications. Happy reading!

Moor, James H. “Why We Need Better Ethics for Emerging Technologies.” Ethics and Information Technology, vol. 7, no. 3, Sept. 2005, pp. 111–119., doi:https://doi.org/10.1007/s10676-006-0008-0.

Lin, Herbert. "Unleashing the High-tech Dogs of War: Artificial intelligence will bring deadlier, smarter weapons. And the command structures that deploy them are likely to possess fewer scruples about harming civilians." Hoover Digest, no. 2, 2018, p. 77+. Opposing Viewpoints in Context, http://link.galegroup.com/apps/doc/A537492347/OVIC?u=umd_um&sid=OVIC&xid=2393afba. Accessed 30 Sept. 2018.

Etzioni, Amitai, and Oren Etzioni. "Should artificial intelligence be regulated?" Issues in Science and Technology, vol. 33, no. 4, 2017, p. 32+. Opposing Viewpoints in Context, http://link.galegroup.com/apps/doc/A499863892/OVIC?u=umd_um&sid=OVIC&xid=6ac93380. Accessed 30 Sept. 2018.

Frankish, Keith, et al. “The ethics of artificial intelligence: Ethics in machine learning and other domain-specific AI algorithms” The Cambridge Handbook of Artificial Intelligence. Cambridge University Press, 2014.

Bryson, Joanna, and Alan Winfield. “Standardizing Ethical Design for Artificial Intelligence and Autonomous Systems.” Computer (00189162), vol. 50, no. 5, May 2017, pp. 116–119. EBSCOhost, doi:10.1109/MC.2017.154.

Mccarthy, J., and P.j. Hayes. “Some Philosophical Problems from the Standpoint of Artificial Intelligence.” Readings in Artificial Intelligence, 1981, pp. 431–450., doi:10.1016/b978-0-934613-03-3.50033-7.

Dirican, Cüneyt. “The Impacts of Robotics, Artificial Intelligence On Business and Economics.” Procedia - Social and Behavioral Sciences, vol. 195, 2015, pp. 564–573., doi:10.1016/j.sbspro.2015.06.134.

Chien, S., et al. “The Future of AI in Space.” IEEE Intelligent Systems, vol. 21, no. 4, 2006, pp. 64–69., doi:10.1109/mis.2006.79.

Mcarthur, David, et al. “The Roles Of Artificial Intelligence In Education: Current Progress And Future Prospects.” i-Manager's Journal of Educational Technology, vol. 1, no. 4, 2005, pp. 42–80., doi:10.26634/jet.1.4.972.

Pennachin, Cassio and Goertzel, Ben. Artificial General Intelligence. Springer, 2007.
